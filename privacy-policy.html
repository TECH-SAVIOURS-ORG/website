Additional information


How we protect your data

Our devices, which we’re sending, writing to you with your personal details (email traffic, text, questions etc.) are encrypted. That means, if someone steal or if we loose our devices it’ll be hard for everyone to get access to our data/your data. We’re using Linux at our Desktop devices which are encrypted with LUKS. And we’re using Andorid and Linux Phones which are encrypted as well and free of Google with Open Source Software. If we’re using proprietary software they’re blocked with a firewall or hasn’t any internet aspiration.

The servers are also encrypted with LUKS.
